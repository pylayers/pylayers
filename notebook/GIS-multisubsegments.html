The different steps are :

  • determine the signatures
  • determine the 2d rays
  • determine the 3d rays
  • determine local basis on 3D rays
  • fill interactions
Si = Signatures(S.L,ctx,crx)
Si.run4(cutoff=5)
r2d = Si.rays(tx,rx)
r3d = r2d.to3D(S.L)
r3d.locbas(S.L)
